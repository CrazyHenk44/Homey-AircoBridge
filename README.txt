Connect air conditioners exposed by AircoBridge to Homey over your local network.

Control power, target temperature, operating mode, fan speed and vane positions. The app also exposes indoor and outdoor temperatures, device alerts, current power use and cumulative energy consumption.

This app requires a running AircoBridge instance that is reachable from Homey. During pairing, enter the bridge URL and select the air conditioner you want to add.

Airco Bridge is a community integration and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Mitsubishi Heavy Industries.
